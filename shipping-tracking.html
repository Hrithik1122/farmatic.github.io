SHIPPING,TRACKING & RETURNS

We do not accept returns of the items sold under a promotion or stock clearance. The product description page will clearly state that you will not be able to return such an item unless it arrives in a damaged condition or is defective.

SHIPPING POLICY

Farmatic & Gopal Trading Company offers free shipping on all orders. For total orders below Rs.999, Rs.55 is charged. COD charges of Rs.45 as convenience charges are applied to all cash on delivery orders. Order dispatch usually takes just one day if the product is in stock, else the lead time depends upon the type of product which will be notified to the customer. Shipping duration usually takes one week but may take longer, depending upon your location. Customers usually receive their packages within 3 to 5 working days, once they are shipped. This delivery period may be longer for more remote locations. If you have any questions about the expected delivery period, please call us at +91 9890-097-996 or email us at support@gopaltrade.com or help@farmatic.co.in



Tracking / where’s My Order

Please Note: Farmatic & Gopal Trading Company discounted and offers have a dispatch time of approx. 10 working days. you can only track your order if you created an account at the time of ordering or have previously created an account. To track the progress of your order, please log in to your Farmatic or Gopal Trading Company account and review your Order History. There are 3 order statuses:

Pending

We have received your order and the artwork file is in the process of generation.

Processing

Your order is in the process of being manufactured or is complete and awaiting shipment.

Shipped

Your order has been shipped to you. See Domestic Shipping for more details.

Returns / Refund Policy

What is your policy on returns?

If the Farmatic or Gopal Trading Company product received by you is in a damaged or defective condition, you may return the Farmatic or Gopal Trading Company product to us within 14 days of receipt, and we will provide a replacement of the product, No refund of the product will be Provided. In all cases, you must return the Farmatic or Gopal Trading Company unused with a copy of the dispatch note.

Return costs are not pre-paid. Postage or other return costs will be the customer's responsibility. You must return the goods, together with their packaging, in the same condition as supplied to you. Returns are sent at your risk.

The colors and images on-screen appear in RGB color format whereas the printing and manufacturing are done in CMYK colors, so there may be a slight difference between an onscreen product and the printed or manufactured one, so please do check these carefully, as colors can appear differently on different monitors.

The image quality is not as good as expected

When you are uploading artwork it is your responsibility to supply us with an image that is of good enough quality. This is further made apparent when using the designer tool. Artwork uploading and printing are fully automated and are not checked.

The delivery I gave was incorrect Once dispatched, if the order is returned to us by the registered post due to either an insufficient or incorrect address being entered, we will notify you, and should you request that the order be re-dispatched we will send a payment request for the postage charge again. The order will not be re-dispatched until this payment is received.

What to do if the item is faulty if the product we have sent you is faulty or damaged please do not hesitate to contact us at: return@gopaltrade.com or help@farmatic.co.in
